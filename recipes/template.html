GRILLED CHICKEN FAJITAS


Serves 4

Requires:

  1. 1.5 lbs Chicken Breasts
  2. 3 Bell Peppers
  3. 1 Red Onion
  4. 2 Tbsp Chili Powder
  5. 2 Tbsp Cumin
  6. 1 tsp Garlic Powder
  7. 1 tsp Onion Powder
  8. 1 tsp Salt


Directions

Step 1

Cut chicken breasts, bell peppers, and onion into strips.

Step 2

Mix spices together in a bowl.

Step 3

Toss spices, chicken, and vegetables together and wrap them in foil.

Step 4

Cook on grill over indirect heat for 20-25 minutes.

Step 5

Serve with tortillas.
