SWEET POTATO TACOS


Serves 8

Requires

  • 2 lbs Ground Beef
  • 4 medium Sweet Potatoes, peeled and chopped small
  • 2 cups Fresh Spinach, chopped
  • 1 Jalapeño, seeded, membranes removed and finely chopped
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • ½ tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • ½ cup Onion, finely chopped
  • ½ Tbsp chili powder
  • 2 tsps Coriander, ground
  • ⅓ cup Water
  • 30 oz Beans, rinsed and drained
  • Garnishes to taste


Directions

Step 1

In a large 12-inch nonstick skillet over the campfire, add the ground meat, salt, pepper, and onion. Cook, breaking the meat into small pieces, until the meat is cooked through. Drain any excess grease.

Step 2

Add the chili powder, cumin, coriander, tomato sauce and jalapeño (if using). Stir for 2-3 minutes. Stir in the water, spinach and beans and simmer for 4-5 minutes. Remove from the fire.

Step 3

Toss spices, chicken, and vegetables together and wrap them in foil.

Step 4

Lightly grease the center of 6-8 foil pieces, each about 12-inches long.

Step 5

Place about 1 cup of sweet potato chunks in the center of each piece of foil arranging them into somewhat of a single layer; sprinkle with salt and pepper (don’t skip this step!). Add a heaping 1/2 cup or so of the meat mixture on top. Bring the long sides of foil together over the filling and fold tightly down. Fold and crimp each edge until tightly sealed and a little packet is formed. Repeat with all the pieces of foil and ingredients.

Step 6

Place the foil packets near the fire for 20-25 minutes.

Step 7

Serve with taco toppings and garnishes of your choice.
