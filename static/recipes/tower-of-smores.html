S'MORES TOWERS


Serves 3

Requires

  • 6 Cookies
  • 3 Marshmallows
  • Sliced Strawberries
  • Chocolate Squares
  • Cinnamon Powder, to taste


Directions

Step 1

Skewer the marshmallows about 12 inches from the campfire.

Step 2

Rotate the marshmallows continually for 3 to 5 minutes.

Step 3

Assemble the s'mores! Add generous amounts of cinnamon and your favorite toppings.
