BAKED SHRIMP


Serves 6

Requires

  • 2 lbs Large Shrimp, peeled
  • 4 Chicken Sausage Links, precooked and sliced
  • 2 ears of Corn, cut in half
  • 4 Red Potatoes, quartered
  • 1 Squash, sliced
  • 2 Tbsps Oilve Oil
  • 4 tsps Salt
  • 1 Lemon, sliced


Directions

Step 1

Cut four sheets of foil, about 12-inches long. Divide shrimp, sausage, corn, potatoes and squash into 4 equal portions and add to the center of each foil in a single layer.

Step 2

Fold up all 4 sides of each foil packet. Add olive oil, salt and 2-3 lemon slices to taste; gently toss to combine. Fold the sides of the foil over the shrimp, covering completely and sealing the packets closed.

Step 3

Place foil packets on the campfire and cook until just cooked through, about 12-15 minutes.

Step 4

Cook on grill over indirect heat for 20-25 minutes.
